Welcome to Sleep Tracker!


You can log your daytime sleepiness by choosing from a range of value that best describes how you felt during a particular time of the day. You can also log your overnight sleep by starting a log before you sleep and ending it after you wake up or logging everything at once after you wake up.

Get started at the Log tab.


Data you log will appear in the View tab.
You can view both your daytime sleepiness logs and your overnight sleep logs in a single list. Click on each log to view more detailed information about that log.
